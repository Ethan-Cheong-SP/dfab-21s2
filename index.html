What is the purpose of this website?

Hi! Welcome to my Website for Digital Fabrication and Prototyping Fundamentals Module (EP1000). This website is meant to serve as documentation for my Journey through the Digital Fabrication and Prototyping Fundamentals Module (EP1000). I will be documenting little projects and assignments I have done during this module as well as My Final Project.

What is EP1000?

The elective is organised around a self-directed project where students develop design thinking and prototyping skills. Students will be guided through a design thinking process followed by the development of a functional prototype. Students will apply design and digital fabrication process for developing a functional prototype that integrates these processes. Students will practise these skills in a design studio setting.
